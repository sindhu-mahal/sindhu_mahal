About Us

sindu Mahal A/C is located at Porur,Mount poonamallee Main Road (Opp to Ramachandra Hospital & Near Hotel Ashok Residency) in Chennai. It has spacious dining hall, modern kitchen with sophisticated equipment and A/c Rooms. The amenities here are excellent and incomparable. It is architect designed Kalyana Mandapam. A hall for Weddings, Betrothal, engagements, Birthday parties, Get-togethers, Exhibitions, Seminars, Conferences etc.,
